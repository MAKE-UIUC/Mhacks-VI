Hello There

StreamPoint presentations allows you to create presentations on the fly

Streams

Whats a presentation without a doc cam? Our mobile app can take advantage of IOS mobile and stream video of you doing anything you want!.

Web App

The presentation is built on a web stack with a Flask backend and angular frontend.

In addition to presentation mode, the web app allows you to add generic preferred settings to your slides.

Fragments

Hit the next arrow...

... to step through ...

... a fragmented slide.

This slide has fragments which are also stepped through in the mobile app.

Fragment Styles

There's different types of presentation styles, like:

grow

shrink

fade-out

current-visible

highlight-blue

Slide Backgrounds

Set data-background="#dddddd" on a slide to change the background color. All CSS color formats are supported.

Dynamic Image Backgrounds

<section data-background="image.png">

Dynamic Tiled Backgrounds

<section data-background="image.png" data-background-repeat="repeat" data-background-size="100px">

Dynamic Video Backgrounds / Streams

<section data-background-video="video.mp4,video.webm">

... and GIFs!

Background Transitions

Different background transitions are available via the backgroundTransition option. This one's called "zoom". 

Reveal.configure({ backgroundTransition: 'zoom' })

Background Transitions

You can override background transitions per-slide on the mobile app. 

<section data-background-transition="zoom">

Speaker View

There's a speaker view. It includes a timer, preview of the upcoming slide as well as your speaker notes.

Press the S key to try it out.

Oh hey, these are some notes. They'll be hidden in your presentation, but you can see them if you open the speaker notes window (hit 's' on your keyboard).

Blank the Slides.

Press B on your keyboard to blank the presentation. This is helpful when you're on stage and want to take distracting slides off the screen.
